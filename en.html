What is franchising?

Franchise is a form of association of owners in a large team, with the ability to use the infrastructure, promote a trademark and work in a team of professionals, minimize risks, go directly to a high level.
Franchising provides many opportunities for network development. The world's largest companies, such as McDonald's, KFC, Radisson Blu, have been using franchising for decades. ➡ Without franchising they would not be today who they are (for example, more than 80% of McDonald's restaurants are franchised).

Franchising, as a system, has proven itself as a way to quickly promote your business. As shown by unbiased statistics, the use of such a system allows 80% to overcome the five-year milestone in the development of the company and continue to work with obtaining high profits. For comparison: the majority of self-propelled firms barely reach two years.
